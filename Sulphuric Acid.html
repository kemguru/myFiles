  • Manufacture of Sulphuric Acid (Contact Process)

    • In the first step, Sulphur is burnt in air to form Sulphur dioxide gas:

      • S + O2 → SO2

               

    • In the next step, Sulphur dioxide is mixed with air and passed over hot Vanadium Pentoxide catalyst at 450oC and 1 atmosphere pressure to form Sulphur trioxide:

      • SO2 + O2  ⇌   SO3

               

    • In the next step, Sulphur trioxide is absorbed in concentrated Sulphuric acid to form Oleum (H2S2O7):

      • SO3 + H2SO4→ H2S2O7

               

    • In the final step,water is added to Oleum to form concentrated Sulphuric acid:

      • H2S2O7 + H2O→ H2SO4

               

  • Reactions of dilute Sulphuric Acid (As an acid):

    1. When Magnesium metal is added to dilute Sulphuric acid, bubbles of Hydrogen gas are liberated:

      2. Mg + H2SO4 → MgSO4 + H2

               

    2. When Zinc metal is added to dilute Sulphuric acid, bubbles of Hydrogen gas are liberated:

      3. Zn + H2SO4 → ZnSO4 + H2

              

    3. When Iron metal is added to dilute Sulphuric acid, bubbles of Hydrogen gas are liberated:

      4. Fe + H2SO4 → FeSO4 + H2

              

    4. Magnesium oxide reacts with dilute Sulphuric acid to form Magnesium sulphate and water:

      5. MgO + H2SO4 → MgSO4 + H2O

               

    5. Aluminium oxide reacts with dilute Sulphuric acid to form Aluminium sulphate and water:

      6. Al2O3 + H2SO4 → Al2(SO4)3 + H2O

               

    6. Zinc oxide reacts with dilute Sulphuric acid to form Zinc sulphate and water:

      7. ZnO + H2SO4 → ZnSO4 + H2O

               

    7. Iron (III) oxide reacts with dilute Sulphuric acid to form Iron (III) sulphate and water:

      8. Fe2O3 + H2SO4 → Fe2(SO4)3 + H2O

              

    8. Copper (II) oxide reacts with dilute Sulphuric acid to form Copper (II) sulphate and water:

      9. CuO + H2SO4 → CuSO4 + H2O

               

    9. Ammonia gas reacts with dilute Sulphuric acid to form Ammonium sulphate:

      10. NH3 + H2SO4 → (NH4)2SO4

              

    10. dilute Sulphuric acid reacts with Potassium hydroxide to form Potassium sulphate and water:

      11. H2SO4 + KOH → K2SO4 + H2O

               

    11. dilute Sulphuric acid reacts with Sodium hydroxide to form Sodium sulphate and water:

      12. H2SO4 + NaOH → Na2SO4 + H2O

               

    12. dilute Sulphuric acid reacts with Calcium hydroxide to form Calcium sulphate and water:

      13. H2SO4 + Ca(OH)2 → CaSO4 + H2O

               

    13. dilute Sulphuric acid reacts with white Magnesium hydroxide to form colourless Magnesium sulphate and water:

      14. H2SO4 + Mg(OH)2 → MgSO4 + H2O

               

    14. dilute Sulphuric acid reacts with white Aluminium hydroxide to form colourless Aluminium sulphate and water:

      15. H2SO4 + Al(OH)3 → Al2(SO4)3 + H2O

               

    15. dilute Sulphuric acid reacts with white Zinc hydroxide to form colourless Zinc sulphate and water:

      16. H2SO4 + Zn(OH)2 → ZnSO4 + H2O

               

    16. dilute Sulphuric acid reacts with green Iron(II) hydroxide to form light green Iron(II) sulphate and water:

      17. H2SO4 + Fe(OH)2 → FeSO4 + H2O

               

    17. dilute Sulphuric acid reacts with reddish brown Iron(III)hydroxide to form yellow Iron(III) sulphate and water:

      18. H2SO4 + Fe(OH)3 → Fe2(SO4)3 + H2O

               

    18. dilute Sulphuric acid reacts with blue copper(II) hydroxide to form blue copper(II)sulphate and water:

      19. H2SO4 + Cu(OH)2 → CuSO4 + H2O

               

    19. Potassium carbonate reacts with dilute Sulphuric acid to form Potassium sulphate, carbon dioxide and water:

      20. K2CO3+ H2SO4 →K2SO4+ CO2+H2O

               

    20. Sodium carbonate reacts with dilute Sulphuric acid to form Sodium sulphate, carbon dioxide and water:

      21. Na2CO3+ H2SO4 →Na2SO4+ CO2+ H2O

               

    21. Calcium carbonate reacts with dilute Sulphuric acid to form Calcium sulphate, carbon dioxide and water:

      22. CaCO3 + H2SO4 → CaSO4+ CO2+H2O

               

    22. Magnesium carbonate reacts with dilute Sulphuric acid to form Magnesium sulphate, carbon dioxide and water:

      23. MgCO3+ H2SO4 → MgSO4+ CO2+H2O

               

    23. Zinc carbonate reacts with dilute Sulphuric acid to form Zinc sulphate, carbon dioxide and water:

      24. ZnCO3+ H2SO4 →ZnSO4+ CO2+H2O

               

    24. Copper (II) carbonate reacts with dilute Sulphuric acid to form Copper (II) sulphate, carbon dioxide and water:

      25. CuCO3+ H2SO4 →CuSO4+ CO2+H2O

               

  • Reactions of concentrated Sulphuric Acid (As an oxidizing agent)

    1. Hot concentrated Sulphuric acid oxidizes Copper metal into Copper(II)sulphate:

      2. Cu + H2SO4 → CuSO4 + SO2 + H2O

               

    2. Hot concentrated Sulphuric acid oxidizes Carbon into Carbon dioxide gas:

      3. C + H2SO4 → CO2 + SO2 + H2O

               

    3. Hot concentrated Sulphuric acid oxidizes Sulphur into Sulphur dioxide:

      4. S + H2SO4 → SO2 + H2O

               

  • Reactions of concentrated Sulphuric Acid (As a dehydrating agent)

    1. Concentrated Sulphuric acid dehydrates sugar into Carbon:

      2. C12H22O11 → C + H2O

               

    2. Concentrated Sulphuric acid dehydrates blue crystals of hydrated copper (II) sulphate into white anhydrous copper (II) sulphate powder:

      3. CuSO4.5H2O → CuSO4 H2O

               

  • Reactions of concentrated Sulphuric Acid (As a less volatile acid)

    1. Concentrated Sulphuric acid reacts with Potassium chloride to form Hydrogen chloride gas and Potassium bisulphate:

      2. KCl + H2SO4 → HCl+ KHSO4

               

    2. Concentrated Sulphuric acid reacts with Sodium chloride to form Hydrogen chloride gas and Sodium bisulphate:

      3. NaCl + H2SO4 → HCl+ NaHSO4

               

    3. Concentrated Sulphuric acid reacts with Potassium nitrate to form Nitric acid and Potassium bisulphate:

      4. KNO3 + H2SO4 → HNO3 KHSO4

               

    4. Concentrated Sulphuric acid reacts with Sodium nitrate to form Nitric acid and Sodium bisulphate:

      5. NaNO3 + H2SO4 → HNO3+ NaHSO4
