Practise balancing equations:

Direction:

  • In the following equations you will see a blank box before the symbol of each compound and element. Enter the lowest possible number in each box, or leave the box blank if you think the chemical needn't have a number (i.e. the number is equivalent to '1').
  • When you think the equation is balanced, click on the small button "Check answer" that appears below it.
  • If your equation is incorrect, then click the button "Reset" and try balancing the equation again.
  • In case you are in a hurry or are unable to balance, then click the button "Show answer."
  • If you want to know the time taken (minutes:seconds) to balance a chemical equation, you click the "Start" button and after balancing the equation, click the "Stop"button. Once, you have noted the time, click the "Reset Timer" button.

Preparation of Carbon Monoxide(Laboratory Methods):

1. When Methanoic(Formic)acid is heated gently with concentrated Sulphuric acid, Carbon monoxide and water are formed:

HCOOH →CO + H2O

2.When Oxalic acid is heated with concentrated Sulphuric acid, Carbon dioxide, Carbon monoxide and water are formed. Carbon dioxide is removed by passing the gases through concentrated Potassium hydroxide solution:

H2C2O4 →CO2 + CO + H2O

3.When Carbon dioxide gas is passed over red hot coke(Carbon), Carbon monoxide gas is formed:

CO2 + C → CO

Reactions of Carbon monoxide:

1. Carbon monoxide gas burns in Oxygen with a blue flame to form carbon dioxide gas:

CO + O2 →CO2

         

Time Taken:00:00

2.When a mixture of Carbon monoxide and steam is passed over Iron(III) oxide(catalyst) at 450oC,Carbon dioxide and Hydrogen are formed.

CO + H2O →CO2 + H2

         

Time Taken:00:00

3. In the presence of sunlight, Carbon monoxide reacts with Chlorine gas to form Phosgene(carbonyl Chloride):

CO + Cl2 →COCl2

         

Time Taken:00:00

4. Carbon monoxide reacts with heated yellow Zinc oxide to form grey Zinc metal and Carbon dioxide:

CO + ZnO →Zn+ CO2

         

Time Taken:00:00

5. Carbon monoxide reacts with heated brown Iron(III) oxide to form greyish black Iron metal and Carbon dioxide:

CO + Fe2O3 →Fe+ CO2

         

Time Taken:00:00

6. Carbon monoxide reacts with heated orange Lead(II) oxide to form grey Lead metal and Carbon dioxide:

CO + PbO →Pb+ CO2

         

Time Taken:00:00

7. Carbon monoxide reacts with heated black Copper(II) oxide to form brown Copper metal and Carbon dioxide:

CO + CuO →Cu+ CO2

         

Time Taken:00:00
