Manufacture of Hydrogen:

1. In Bosch Process, white hot coke at about 1000oC is reacted with steam when water gas(CO + H2) is formed:

C + H2O → CO + H2

         

Time Taken:00:00

  • Next,the mixture of Carbon monoxide and Hydrogen is mixed with more steam and passed over heated Iron(III)oxide(catalyst) at 450oC when Carbon dioxide and Hydrogen are formed:

CO + H2 + H2O → CO2 + H2

         

Time Taken:00:00

  • Carbon dioxide is removed by passing the mixture through Potassium hydroxide solution when Carbon dioxide reacts with Potassium hydroxide forming Potassium carbonate and water:

CO2 + KOH → K2CO3 + H2O

         

Time Taken:00:00

2. In Modern Process,a mixture of Methane (present in natural gas) and steam is passed over heated Nickel(catalyst)at about 800oC when synthesis gas(CO + 3 H2) is formed:

CH4 + H2O → CO + H2

         

Time Taken:00:00

  • Next,the mixture of Carbon monoxide and Hydrogen is mixed with more steam and passed over heated Iron(III)oxide(catalyst) at 450oC when Carbon dioxide and Hydrogen are formed:

CO + H2 + H2O → CO2 + H2

         

Time Taken:00:00

  • Carbon dioxide is removed by passing the mixture through Potassium hydroxide solution when Carbon dioxide reacts with Potassium hydroxide forming Potassium carbonate and water:

CO2 + KOH → K2CO3 + H2O

         

Time Taken:00:00

Reactions of Hydrogen:

1. A mixture of Hydrogen and Oxygen, when ignited, burns with a 'pop' sound to form water:

H2 + O2 → H2O

         

Time Taken:00:00

2. In diffused sunlight Hydrogen reacts with Chlorine to form Hydrogen chloride gas:

H2 + Cl2 → HCl

         

Time Taken:00:00

3. When bubbled through molten Sulphur, Hydrogen gas reacts with Sulphur to form Hydrogen sulphide gas:

H2 + S → H2S

         

Time Taken:00:00

4.When A mixture of Nitrogen gas and Hydrogen gas is passed over Iron catalyst at 200 atm pressure and 450oC, Ammonia gas is formed:

N2 + H2  ⇌    NH3

        

Time Taken:00:00

5. When Hydrogen gas is passed over heated black Copper(II) oxide, brown Copper and water are formed:

H2 + CuO → Cu + H2O

         

Time Taken:00:00

6. When Hydrogen gas is passed over heated yellow Lead(II) oxide, grey Lead and water are formed:

H2 + PbO → Pb + H2O

         

Time Taken:00:00

7. When Hydrogen gas is passed over heated brown Iron(III) oxide, grey Iron and water are formed:

H2 + Fe2O3 → Fe + H2O

         

Time Taken:00:00
