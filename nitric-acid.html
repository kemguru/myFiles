Preparation of Nitric acid:

1. When Potassium nitrate is heated with concentrated sulphuric acid, nitric acid is formed:

KNO3 + H2SO4→ KHSO4 + HNO3

2.When Sodium Nitrate is  heated with concentrated sulphuric acid, niric acid is formed:

NaNO3 + H2SO4→ NaHSO4 + HNO3

        

Time Taken:00:00

Manufacture of Nitric acid (Ostwald's Process):

  • When a mixture of ammonia gas and air is passed over Platinum catalyst at 700oCand 5 atmosphere pressure, nitrogen monoxide (nitric oxide) is formed.

NH3 + O2  ⇌   NO  + H2O

        

Time Taken:00:00

  • Nitrogen monoxide then reacts with oxygen at low temperature to form nitrogen dioxide gas:

NO + O2→ NO2

         

Time Taken:00:00

  • Nitrogen dioxide then reacts with water and oxygen to form Nitric acid:

NO2 + H2O + O2 → HNO3

         

Time Taken:00:00

Reactions of concentrated Nitric acid (As an oxidizing agent):

1. Concentrated Nitric acid reacts with Copper metal to form Copper(II)nitrate, Nitrogen dioxide gas which is acidic, pungent smelling and reddish brown in colour, and water:

HNO3 + Cu → Cu(NO3)2 + NO2 + H2O

         

Time Taken:00:00

2.Concentrated Nitric acid reacts with Carbon to form carbon dioxide gas, Nitrogen dioxide gas which is acidic, pungent smelling and reddish brown in colour, and water:

HNO3 + C → CO2 + NO2 + H2O

         

Time Taken:00:00

3.Concentrated Nitric acid reacts with Sulphur to form Sulphuric acid, Nitrogen dioxide gas which is acidic, pungent smelling and reddish brown in colour, and water:

HNO3 + S → H2SO4 + NO2 + H2O

         

Time Taken:00:00

Effect of heat on metal Nitrates:

1. When Potassium nitrate is heated strongly it decomposes to form Potassium nitrite and Oxygen gas:

KNO3 → KNO2 + O2

         

Time Taken:00:00

2. When Sodium nitrate is heated strongly it decomposes to form Sodium nitrite and Oxygen gas:

NaNO3 → NaNO2 + O2

         

Time Taken:00:00

3. When Calcium nitrate is heated strongly it decomposes to form white Calcium oxide, Nitrogen dioxide gas which is acidic, pungent smelling and reddish brown in colour and Oxygen gas:

Ca(NO3)2 → CaO + NO2 + O2

         

Time Taken:00:00

4. When Magnesium nitrate is heated strongly it decomposes to form white Magnesium oxide, Nitrogen dioxide gas which is acidic, pungent smelling and reddish brown in colour and Oxygen gas:

Mg(NO3)2 → MgO + NO2 + O2

         

Time Taken:00:00

5. When Aluminium nitrate is heated strongly it decomposes to form white Aluminium oxide, Nitrogen dioxide gas which is acidic, pungent smelling and reddish brown in colour and Oxygen gas:

Al(NO3)3 → Al2O3 + NO2 + O2

         

Time Taken:00:00

6. When Zinc nitrate is heated strongly it decomposes to form zinc oxide which is yellow when hot and white when cold, Nitrogen dioxide gas which is acidic, pungent smelling and reddish brown in colour and Oxygen gas:

Zn(NO3)2 → ZnO + NO2 + O2

         

Time Taken:00:00

7. When Lead(II) nitrate is heated strongly it decomposes to form yellow Lead(II)oxide, Nitrogen dioxide gas which is acidic, pungent smelling and reddish brown in colour and Oxygen gas:

Pb(NO3)2 → PbO + NO2 + O2

         

Time Taken:00:00

8. When blue Copper(II) nitrate is heated strongly it decomposes to form black Copper(II)oxide, Nitrogen dioxide gas which is acidic, pungent smelling and reddish brown in colour and Oxygen gas:

Cu(NO3)2 → CuO + NO2 + O2

         

Time Taken:00:00

9. When Ammonium nitrate is heated strongly it decomposes to form Nitrous oxide (Laughing gas)and water:

NH4NO3 → N2O + H2O

         

Time Taken:00:00

10. When Ammonium nitrite is heated strongly it explodes to form nitrogen and water:

NH4NO2 → N2 + H2O

         

Time Taken:00:00
