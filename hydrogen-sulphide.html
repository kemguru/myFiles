Hydrogen Sulphide

Practise balancing equations:

Direction:

  • In the following equations you will see a blank box before the symbol of each compound and element. Enter the lowest possible number in each box, or leave the box blank if you think the chemical needn't have a number (i.e. the number is equivalent to '1').
  • When you think the equation is balanced, click on the small button "Check answer" that appears below it.
  • If your equation is incorrect, then click the button "Reset" and try balancing the equation again.
  • In case you are in a hurry or are unable to balance, then click the button "Show answer."
  • If you want to know the time taken (minutes:seconds) to balance a chemical equation, you click the "Start" button and after balancing the equation, click the "Stop"button. Once, you have noted the time, click the "Reset Timer" button.

Note:

If you get no response after clicking 'Check answer' or 'Show answer', your browser has disabled to run this program. Enable it by clicking below the 'address bar' and follow the instructions.

Preparation of Hydrogen Sulphide:

1. When Iron (II)Sulphide is reacted with dilute Hydrochloric acid, Hydrogen sulphide gas is formed:

FeS + HCl → FeCl2+ H2S

2. When Iron (II)Sulphide is reacted with dilute Sulphuric acid, Hydrogen sulphide gas is formed:

FeS + H2SO4→ FeSO4+ H2S

Reactions of Hydrogen Sulphide:

1. Hydrogen sulphide gas burns in limited amount of air to form Sulphur and water:

H2S + O2→ S + H2O

         

Time Taken:00:00

2. In excess air Hydrogen sulphide gas burns with a blue flame to form Sulphur dioxide and water:

H2S + O2→ SO2 + H2O

         

Time Taken:00:00

3. Hydrogen sulphide gas reacts with Chlorine gas to form Sulphur and Hydrogen chloride gas:

H2S + Cl2→ S + HCl

         

Time Taken:00:00

4.Hydrogen sulphide gas reacts with Lead (II) nitrate solution to form black precipitate of Lead (II) sulphide:

H2S + Pb(NO3)2→ PbS + HNO3

         

Time Taken:00:00

5.Hydrogen sulphide gas reacts with Copper (II) sulphate solution to form brownish black precipitate of Copper(II) sulphide:

H2S + CuSO4→ PbS + H2SO4

         

Time Taken:00:00

6.Hydrogen sulphide gas reacts with Zinc sulphate solution to form white precipitate of Zinc sulphide:

H2S + ZnSO4→ ZnS + H2SO4

         

Time Taken:00:00

7.Hydrogen sulphide gas reduces Sulphur dioxide gas into Sulphur:

H2S + SO2→ S + H2O

         

Time Taken:00:00

8.Hydrogen sulphide gas reduces Iron(III)chloride to Iron(II)chloride:

FeCl3 + H2S → FeCl2 + HCl + S

        

Time Taken:00:00

9.Hydrogen sulphide gas reduces Nitric acid to reddish brown Nitrogen dioxide gas:

HNO3 + H2S → H2O + NO2 + S

        

Time Taken:00:00
