Manufacture of Sulphuric Acid (Contact Process)

  • In the first step, Sulphur is burnt in air to form Sulphur dioxide gas:

S + O2 →SO2

         

Time Taken:00:00

  • In the next step, Sulphur dioxide is mixed with air and passed over hot Vanadium Pentoxide catalyst at 450oC and 1 atmosphere pressure to form Sulphur trioxide:

SO2 + O2  ⇌  SO3

         

Time Taken:00:00

  • In the next step, Sulphur trioxide is absorbed in concentrated Sulphuric acid to form Oleum (H2S2O7):

SO3 + H2SO4→H2S2O7

         

Time Taken:00:00

  • In the final step,water is added to Oleum to form concentrated Sulphuric acid:

H2S2O7 + H2O→H2SO4

         

Time Taken:00:00

Time Taken:00:00

2.Hot concentrated Sulphuric acid oxidizes Carbon into Carbon dioxide gas:

C + H2SO4 →CO2 + SO2 + H2O

         

Time Taken:00:00
